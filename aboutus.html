About Bhutan Travel

Embark on a journey to the Land of the Thunder Dragon

Who We Are

At Bhutan Travel, we bring you closer to the heart of the Land of the Thunder Dragon. Our expertly curated tours offer a seamless blend of cultural immersion, thrilling adventures, and breathtaking landscapes.

Established with a passion for showcasing Bhutan’s untouched beauty, we have spent years crafting unforgettable travel experiences. Whether you seek the serenity of ancient monasteries, the thrill of trekking through the Himalayas, or the warmth of Bhutanese hospitality, we ensure every moment is truly magical.

Our guides are local experts with in-depth knowledge of Bhutan’s history, traditions, and hidden gems. With their expertise, you’ll explore sacred temples, bustling local markets, and remote mountain trails, all while gaining a deeper understanding of Bhutan’s unique philosophy of Gross National Happiness.

Bhutan Travel believes in responsible and sustainable tourism. We prioritize eco-friendly practices, support local communities, and promote ethical travel to preserve the cultural and natural heritage of our nation.

Paro Taktsang

Perched on the cliffs of Paro Valley, Bhutan, Paro Taktsang, or the Tiger's Nest Monastery, offers a stunning view of the Himalayas. Revered as a spiritual landmark, it holds deep historical significance, attracting visitors with its mystic allure.

Discover More

Bumthang Kurjey Lhakhang

Situated in Bumthang Valley, Bhutan, Bumthang Kurjey Lhakhang is a sacred site where Guru Rinpoche meditated. The monastery is revered for its spiritual significance and tranquil surroundings.

Discover More

Dochula Pass, Thimphu

Dochula Pass, located near Thimphu, Bhutan, offers stunning views of the Himalayan mountains. Known for its 108 stupas, the pass is a peaceful spot for reflection and a gateway to Bhutan's natural beauty.
