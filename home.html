Paro Taktsang

Perched on the cliffs of Paro Valley, Bhutan, Paro Taktsang, or the Tiger's Nest Monastery, offers a stunning view of the Himalayas. Revered as a spiritual landmark, it holds deep historical significance, attracting visitors with its mystic allure.

Discover More

Bumthang Kurjey Lhakhang

Situated in Bumthang Valley, Bhutan, Bumthang Kurjey Lhakhang is a sacred site where Guru Rinpoche meditated. The monastery is revered for its spiritual significance and tranquil surroundings.

Discover More

Dochula Pass, Thimphu

Dochula Pass, located near Thimphu, Bhutan, offers stunning views of the Himalayan mountains. Known for its 108 stupas, the pass is a peaceful spot for reflection and a gateway to Bhutan's natural beauty.
